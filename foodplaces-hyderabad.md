# Top 10 Unexplored Food Places in Hyderabad

Hyderabad is famous for its Biryani, but there are many hidden gems that offer unique and mouth-watering flavors. Here are 10 unexplored food places you must visit:

1. **Milan Coffee Bar (Mallepally)**
   - Known for its unique 'Milan Special Coffee' and a variety of snacks, this place is a favorite among locals but remains largely hidden from the mainstream food scene.

2. **Nayab Hotel (Old City)**
   - While well-known to some, its 'Saffron Tea' and 'Bheja Fry' are underrated compared to other big names in the Charminar area.

3. **Subhan Bakery (Nampally)**
   - Famous for its 'Osmania Biscuits' and 'Dum-ka-Roat', this bakery has a legacy that many newcomers to the city have yet to discover.

4. **Akbar Fast Food (Near Charminar)**
   - Specifically known for its 'Chicken 65' and 'Warqi Paratha', it’s a tiny stall that packs a punch with flavors.

5. **Matwale Doodh Ghar (Charminar)**
   - A legendary spot for 'Lassi' and 'Falooda' that offers a refreshing break from the spicy street food of the Old City.

6. **Grand Hotel (Abids)**
   - Often overshadowed by newer chains, their 'Dil Kush' and 'Irani Chai' are timeless classics that deserve more attention.

7. **Govind's Dosa (Gulzar Houz)**
   - A street-side stall that serves incredibly buttery and spicy dosas, which are quite different from the standard South Indian fare.

8. **Pista House (Hafiz Baba Nagar)**
   - While Pista House is famous for Haleem, the original small outlets in the narrower lanes offer an authentic vibe and snacks that are often missed.

9. **Hameedi Confectioners (Mozamjahi Market)**
   - Known for the 'Jouzi Halwa', a rich sweet made with nutmeg and milk, which was a favorite of the Nizams.

10. **Shah Ghouse (Shah Ali Banda)**
    - While its Biryani is popular, the 'Khichdi Khatta Kheema' served for breakfast is a traditional Hyderabadi meal that many tourists overlook.
